Our Story

Founded in 2010, Travel Wanderlust began as a small team of passionate travelers who wanted to share their love for exploration with the world. What started as a blog has grown into a full-service travel agency specializing in curated experiences.

We believe that travel should be transformative, not just transactional. Our mission is to create meaningful connections between travelers and the places they visit, fostering understanding and appreciation for different cultures.

With over 12 years in the industry, we've built strong relationships with local partners worldwide, allowing us to offer exclusive access and unique experiences you won't find anywhere else.
